KALARIPAYATTU

An Indian Martial Art

Kalaripayattu, also known as Kalari, is a martial art form that originated in South India.

It is well-known for its long-standing history and rich tradition.

I started learning this martial art as a hobby 2 years ago, and now it has been part of my lifestyle.
