My jounrney

A few years back, I had an interest in learning martial art. As I explored the various forms, I realised I liked Kalari. Hence, I started my journey with Kalari Academy in 2019.

The master and trainers motivated me and pushed me to do my best. On top of learning the Kalari skills, I honed other skills such as self-discipline, confidence and being more focused. Physically, I can also see myself getting stronger and more flexible.

It is nice seeing something that started as a hobby slowly turning to be part of my lifestyle.
